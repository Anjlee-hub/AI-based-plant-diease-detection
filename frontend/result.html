📖 Description

Early Blight is a fungal disease that affects tomato plants and causes leaf damage.

⚠ Symptoms

  • Brown leaf spots
  • Yellow leaves
  • Leaf drying
  • Reduced plant growth

💊 Recommended Treatment

Organic: Neem Oil Spray


Chemical: Mancozeb Fungicide

🛡 Prevention Tips

  • Avoid overwatering
  • Improve air circulation
  • Remove infected leaves
  • Use healthy seeds
