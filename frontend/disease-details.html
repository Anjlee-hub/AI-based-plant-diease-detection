Tomato Early Blight

📖 Description

A fungal disease affecting tomato plants.

⚠ Symptoms

  • Brown spots on leaves
  • Yellowing of leaves
  • Leaf drying

🌿 Organic Remedies

  • Neem Oil Spray
  • Compost Tea

🧪 Chemical Remedies

  • Mancozeb Fungicide

🛡 Prevention Tips

  • Avoid overwatering
  • Remove infected leaves

🌍 Community Notes

Many farmers reported good results using Neem Oil every 7 days.
